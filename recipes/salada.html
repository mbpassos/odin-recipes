Chicken Salad

Description

This Chicken Salad is a light and refreshing dish, perfect for a healthy lunch or dinner. Made with tender chicken, crisp vegetables, and a creamy dressing, it's easy to prepare and packed with flavor. You can enjoy it on its own or serve it with crackers, bread, or a bed of leafy greens for an extra satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded or cubed
  • 1/2 cup celery, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup grapes or apples, chopped (optional for sweetness)
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or dill (optional, for garnish)
  • Mixed greens or lettuce (for serving, optional)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded or cubed chicken, celery, red onion, and grapes (if using).
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and lemon juice until smooth.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chicken mixture and stir gently to combine until everything is well coated.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, garnish with fresh parsley or dill.
  5. Serve the chicken salad on a bed of mixed greens, lettuce, or with crackers and enjoy!
